Serkan Empire

Overview: This is the native land of the Itri, the place where the sun always shines. The nights are never fully dark. This kingdom has the richest display of nature in all the lands, with bright trees, full grasses, and precious metals laying in abundance just below the surface. Ariunzule is enforced by technological wonders between all the realms. On the outskirts of Ariunzule, they have great bridges connecting them to their neighboring territories.
