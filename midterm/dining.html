Restaurants

Twilight Lounge

This cocktail lounge is where luxury is crafted into an enchanting experience tailored specifically to every individual. This lounge is a beautiful addition to highlight the nightlife experience in Capital Province, with rooftop views of every bright light which shines the twilight hours. Rumors even say that the elusive Monarch of the Veil dined here on the very night of the old Queen of the Sun’s coronation, and it was from this very place that the Monarch of the Veil decided to publicly announce the news of their own coronation by the dawn of the sun’s light upon this lounge. So, come on in and share a drink with the everlasting presence of the grace of the Monarch of the Veil in this lounge of royals .

Diamond Plaza

Witness the richly saturated blend of culture within the heart of the Wrynn territories of Serkan. At this plaza, visitors can journey between the local booth selections of the diverse hybrids of all of the species which live throughout the Empire of Serkan. Furthermore, guests of this plaza can nourish themselves with the empowering fruits and vegetables grown in the traditional ways of the Unicorns of the old kingdom of Wrynn. Legends say that consuming the enchanted and traditionally grown products from Diamond Plaza spreads the healing power of the unicorns to them as well, and can cure any illness or disease.

Cerapter Bistro & Coffee Haven

Named after the winged unicorn, this Bistro and coffee shop of the Wrynn territories of Serkan provides powerful and tasty elixirs for patrons to try of healing magics and variously tailored potions. Additionally, this Bistro is situated on one of the clouded cities which are floating far above the beautiful free fields of Wrynn. Come and join us as we serenely take in the fresh air and the one-in-a-lifetime experience of the skies of the winged unicorns while filling yourself up with delicious food and drinks.
