  3. Various Ways to Establish Hierarchy & Emphasis in Typographic Design

    • When establishing either hierarchy or emphasis by utilizing design with the typography, it’s crucial to pay attention to the size of certain words or even letters so that it can either stand out as in the audience’s face, be so small that the viewer can barely glance at it, or can even just look like an average sized text to act as a supportive filler to back up the parts which are going to be emphasized. Furthermore, the size of the spacing between the lines or even the letters themselves can go to add a sense of hierarchy, as it acts well as an organizational tactic on a layout, as well as adds emphasis if used significantly amongst an array of other different looking text. Additionally, the colors and typeface styles can catch one’s attention too because, for example, utilizing a script font with elegant cursive will most likely stand out significantly amongst the regular looking and plain sans-serif type which can be adorning the rest of the layout.
