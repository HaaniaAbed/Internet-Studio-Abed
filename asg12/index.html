The Great Wall

While searching for gunpowder in China during the Song Dynasty, two European mercenaries- William Garin and Tovar- stumble upon a secret army which defends the Great Wall from a seige imposed by mythical monsters, called the Tao Tei. William Garin offers his aid in the massive battle against the Queen of the Tao Tei and joins forces with the Unnamed Order, alongside the head of the all female Crane Corps, Commander Lin, to save the world.

Green Snake

The movie, Green Snake, is a Chinese animated fantasy action film about the green snake demon, Verta, who is sent to a dystopian cyberpunk city called Asuraville after her sister, Blanca, is captured and trapped under a temple. To survive and find a way to free her sister from the demon-slaying monk Fahai, Verta must navigate and escape from the dangerous city filled with spirits and gang conflicts, through forming unexpected alliances and facing intense battles along the way.

The Watchmen (2009 Film Version)

The Watchmen is an adaptation of the graphic novel set in an alternate 1985 where the Cold War is intensifying, and costumed vigilantes are outlawed. The story begins with the murder of a retired hero, the Comedian, which leads his former colleagues to investigate a potential threat to all vigilantes, including Rorschach, Nite Owl, Silk Spectre II, Ozymandias, and the superpowered Dr. Manhattan.

Ender's Game

Ender's Game is a 2013 science fiction movie about a gifted young boy, Ender Wiggin, who is recruited by the military to train at Battle School to become a leader capable of defending Earth from an alien race called the Formics, which had terrorized Earth in the past.
